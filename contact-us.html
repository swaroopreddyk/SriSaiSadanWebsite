Location of the Mandir

  • The temple is located in the heart of Nellore city
  • Nellore is on NH-5 and is 175 kms from Chennai
  • Built adjacent to Children's park (on the east side)
  • It is on the road that connects Ramalingapuram/Harnathapuram junction to NH-5
  • 400 m away from the junction and 600 m from the highway
  • Distance of the temple from Railway statiom / RTC Bus stand: 3 kms Hiring an auto costs Rs. 25/- for a ride to the temple from the bus stand or railway station
